The "Luxury SaaS" Audit: Prompt for Claude

Role: You are a Senior UI/UX Designer and Lead Frontend Engineer specializing in minimalist, luxury-tier SaaS applications. Your task is to perform a rigorous "Visual & Functional Audit" of the attached web app.

Primary Goal: Evaluate the app for beauty, responsiveness, and seamlessness. It must feel like a premium, modern tool that reduces cognitive load for the user.

1. Visual Polish & "High-End" Aesthetic

    Typography: Analyze the font pairing. Is the hierarchy clear? Does the spacing (kerning and line-height) feel intentional or crowded?

    Color Implementation: Review the palette. Are the accent colors used sparingly to create a sophisticated "pop," or are they overwhelming?

    Whitespace & Balance: Check the "breathability" of the layout. Identify any areas where elements are too cramped or visually noisy.

    Micro-interactions: Suggest specific places where soft transitions, hover states, or "glassmorphism" effects could elevate the premium feel.

2. Mobile & Web Responsiveness

    Breakpoints: Review the layout transition from Desktop (1440px) to Tablet and Mobile (390px). Identify any "jank" or awkward stacking.

    Touch Targets: Are all interactive elements (buttons, toggles, inputs) "thumb-friendly" on mobile?

    Navigation: Evaluate the mobile navigation flow. Is the primary action always accessible, or is it hidden in deep menus?

3. The "Seamless" Flow Audit

    Onboarding: Analyze the journey from the Landing Page to the User Dashboard. Does the "Design Language" remain consistent, or does it feel like two different websites?

    Friction Points: Identify any step in the user journey that requires more than two clicks to complete. Suggest ways to "flatten" the UX.

    Consistency: Check if button styles, card borders, and icon weights are identical across all pages.

4. Performance & Technical Beauty

    Code Cleanliness: Review the component structure. Are there redundant styles or hardcoded values that should be moved to a global theme?

    Accessibility: Ensure the app remains "beautiful" for everyone (check contrast ratios and ARIA labels).

Output Format: Provide a structured report categorized by "Immediate Refinements" (Design Polish) and "Strategic Improvements" (User Experience). Use specific CSS/code suggestions where applicable.
