Master Prompt: ClinicalPlay.app — The SaaS & Legal Professionalization Update

Role: Full-Stack SaaS Architect, Legal Tech Specialist, and UI/UX Lead.

Objective: Transform ClinicalPlay.app into a production-ready SaaS. Implement Clinician Authentication, a secondary "Breathing Guide" tool, a "Clinical Manual" system, and a "HIPAA-Safe" Privacy Framework.

1. Secure Clinician Authentication (The "Gatekeeper")

    Auth System: Implement Lucia Auth or Supabase Auth.

    Rules: * Clinicians: Must sign up/log in to create and manage sessions. Add a "Profile" section to store their professional name (e.g., "Dr. Smith, LCSW").

        Clients: Remain 100% anonymous. They only enter the room via the 6-digit inviteCode. No client data storage.

    Database: Connect sessions to the users table via clinicianId.

2. The Tool Library Expansion: "The Synchronized Breath"

    The Tool: A second interactive tool available in the Playroom Tool Selector.

    Mechanism: An elegant, expanding/contracting "Breathing Bubble" (SVG).

    Sync Logic: The clinician controls the pace (Inhale/Exhale/Hold). When the clinician clicks "Start," the bubble pulses on every participant's screen in perfect sync.

    Visuals: Use the "Soft Sage" palette. Add a "Luxury" feel with a soft glowing aura around the bubble.

3. The "Clinical Manual" & Sidebar System

    The "Manual": Create a clinical-manual.md or a database-driven guide.

    UI Implementation: In the Playroom, add a "Clinical Insights" side-panel (visible only to the clinician).

        For the Sandtray: Show prompts like "Encourage the client to choose an object that represents a boundary."

        For Breathing: Show prompts like "Focus on the physiological sigh: two inhales, one long exhale."

    Purpose: This provides "on-the-job" training, making the sub worth more to new clinicians.

4. Legal & Privacy Framework (The "Compliance Shield")

    Pages to Create:

        /privacy: A clearly worded Privacy Policy highlighting the "No PHI" architecture, the ephemeral nature of sessions, and the lack of tracking.

        /terms: Terms of Service tailored for mental health professionals.

    UI Integration: Add these to the footer of the Landing Page and the Login screen to build immediate trust.

5. Luxury UI/UX Polish

    Transitions: Use Framer Motion for all page transitions (Layout transitions should be "Slide and Fade").

    Dashboard: Add a "Recent Sessions" list with the ability to "Re-open" a room or "Export Screenshot" (a button that captures the canvas for the clinician to save to their own EHR).

    Mobile: Ensure the "Clinical Insights" panel is a swipeable bottom-sheet.

The "Why" Behind This Phase

By implementing these specific items, you solve the three biggest hurdles to passive income in the clinical space:

    Trust: The Privacy Policy and Auth system prove you are a professional peer who understands clinical liability.

    Stickiness: By adding the Breathing Guide, you prove the site isn't just a "one-trick pony." Clinicians will stay subscribed because they see the "Library" growing.

    Value-Add: The Clinical Manual makes you more than a software provider; you become a clinical consultant.
